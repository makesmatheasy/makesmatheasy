Mensuration Formulas For 2D Shapes


\[Shape\] \[Area \space(Square \space units)\] \[Perimeter \space (Units)\] \[Figure\]
\[Square\] \[a^2\] \[4a\]
\[Rectangle\] \[l \space * \space b\] \[2 \space (l + b)\]
\[Circle\] \[\pi r^2\] \[2 \pi r\]
\[Scalene \space Triangle\] \[\sqrt{[s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)]} ,\]
\[where \space s=(a+b+c)/2 \]		 \[a+b+c\]
\[Isosceles \space Triangle\] \[\frac{1}{2} *b *h \] \[2a+b\]
\[Equilateral \space Triangle\] \[\frac{\sqrt{3}}{4} * a^2\] \[3a\]
\[Right \space Angle \space Triangle\] \[\frac{1}{2} *b *h\] \[b+hypotenuse+h\]
\[Rhombous\] \[\frac{1}{2} *d1 *d2\] \[4*Side\]
\[Parallelogram\] \[b*h\] \[2(l+b)\]
\[Trapezium\] \[\frac{1}{2} *h *(a+c)\] \[a+b+c+d\]
