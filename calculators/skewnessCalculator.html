SKEWNESS CALCULATOR

 

 

 

How to Calculate Skewness:
1. Calculate the mean and standard deviation
2. Subtract the mean from each raw score
3. Raise each of these deviations from the mean to the third power and sum
4. Calculate skewness, which is the sum of the deviations from the mean, raise to the third power,
divided by number of cases minus 1, times the standard deviation raised to the third power.
