Negative Binomial Distribution Calculator

The negative binomial distribution calculator, otherwise called the Pascal distribution calculator, helps in determining what is the probability of requiring n trials to achieve a fixed number of successes.


Formula: P(Y=n) = (n-1)C(r-1) * pr * (1-p)(n-r)

where,

n is the total number of trials

r is the number of successes

p is the probability of one success

(n-1)C(r-1) is the number of combinations

P(Y=n) is the probability of the exact number of trials n needed to achieve r successes.
