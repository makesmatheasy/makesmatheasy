Independent and Dependent event Probability

Independent Event Probability

The outcome of one event does not affect the outcome of the other.
If A and B are independent events then probability of both occuring is
P(A and B) = P(A) X P(B)






Dependent Event Probability

The outcome of one event affect the outcome of the other.
If A and B are dependent events then probability of both occuring is
P(A and B) = P(A) X P(B|A)
