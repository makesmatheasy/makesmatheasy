Foil Calculator

FOIL is a method for multiplying two binomials. FOIL is an acronym for the four terms appearing in the final result.

  • F ➺ First terms of each binomial are multiplied.
  • O ➺ Outer terms are multiplied
  • I ➺ Inner terms are multiplied
  • L ➺ Last terms are multiplied.
