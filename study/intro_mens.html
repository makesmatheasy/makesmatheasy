Mensuration

\[Mensuration\,\, is\,\, the\,\, branch\,\, of\,\, mathematics\,\, that\,\, studies\,\, the \]

\[measurement\,\, of\,\, geometric\,\, figures\,\, and\,\, their\,\, parameters\,\, like\,\, length,\]

\[volume,\,\, shape,\,\, surface\,\, area,\,\, lateral\,\, surface\,\, area\,\, etc.\]




Differnce between 2D and 3D shapes


\[2D\] \[3D\]
\[1.\,\, if\,\, a\,\, shape\,\, is\,\, surrounded\,\, by\,\, three\,\, or\,\, more\]
\[straight\,\, lines\,\, in\,\, a\,\, plane,\,\, then\,\, it\,\, is\,\, a\,\, 2D\,\, shape\]		 \[if\,\, a\,\, shape\,\, is\,\, surrounded\,\, by\,\, a\,\, number\,\, of\]
\[surfaces\,\, or\,\, planes\,\, then\,\, it\,\, is\,\, a\,\, 3D\,\, shape\]
\[2.\,\, these\,\, shapes\,\, have\,\, no\,\, depth\,\, or\,\, height\] \[unline\,\, 2D\,\, they\,\, have\,\, height\,\, or\,\, depth\]
\[3.\,\, these\,\, shapes\,\, have\,\, only\,\, two\,\, dimensions\] \[these\,\, shapes\,\, have\,\, three\,\, dimensions\]
\[4.\,\, we\,\, can\,\, measure\,\, their\,\, area\,\, and\,\, perimeter\] \[we\,\, can\,\, measure\,\, their\,\, volume,\,\, CSA\]
\[LSA\,\, or\,\, TSA\]
