Properties of Logarithm



Principle Properties of Logarithm

\[Let\, m\, and\, n\, be\, arbitrary\, positive\, numbers\, ,\, a\,>\,0\, ,\, a\, \mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\, 1\, , \, b\,>\,0\, ,\,b\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,1\,and\space\,α\,,\,β\] \[\,be\,any\,real\,numbers\,,\,then\]

\[1)\space\,log_a\,(mn)\space=\space\,log_a\,m\,+\,log_a\,n\]

\[2)\space\,log_a\,(\,\frac{m}{n}\,)\,\space=\space\,log_a\,m\,-\,log_a\,n\]

\[3)\space\,log_a\,m^α\space=\space\,α\,log_a\,m\]

\[4)\space\,log_{a\space^β}\,m\space=\space\frac{1}{β}\,log_a\,m\]

\[5)\space\,log_b\,m\space=\space\frac{log_a\,m}{log_a\,b}\]

Extra properties of Logarithm

\[1)\space\,a^{{log_b}\,x}\space=\space\,x^{{log_b}\,a}\,,\,b\,\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,1\,,\,a\,,\,b\,,\,x\,are\,positive\,numbers\]

\[2)\space\,a^{{log_a}\,x}\space=\space\,x\,,\,a\,>\,0\,,\,a\,\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,1\,,\,x\,>\,0\]

\[3)\space\,log_{a\space^k}\,x\space=\space\frac{1}{k}\,log_a\,x\,,\,a\,>\,0\,,\,a\,\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,1\,,\,x\,>\,0\]

\[4)\space\,log_a\,x^{2k}\space=\space\,2k\,log_a\,|x|\,,\,a\,>\,0\,,\,a\,\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,1\,,\,k\,\in\,I\]

\[5)\space\,log_{a\space^{2k}}\,x\space=\space\frac{1}{2k}\,log_{|a|}\,x\,,\,x\,>\,0\,,\,a\,>\,0\,,\,a\,\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,±1\space\,and\space\,k\,\in\,I\,\sim\{\,0\}\]

\[6)\space\,log_{a\space^α}\,x^β\space=\space\frac{β}{α}\,log_a\,x\,,\,x\,>\,0\,,\,a\,>\,0\,,\,a\,\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,1\,,\,α\,\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,0\]

\[7)\space\,a^{{log_b}\,a}\space=\space\sqrt{a}\,,\,if\,b\,=\,a^2\,,\,a\,>\,0\,,\,b\,>\,0\,,\,b\,\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,1\]

\[8)\space\,a^{{log_b}\,a}\space=\space\,a^2\,,\,if\,b\,=\sqrt{a}\,,\,a\,>\,0\,,\,b\,>\,0\,,\,b\,\mathrel{\mathtt{!=}}\,1\]
