Welcome,Thanks for Using Makes Math Easy

The app does not work if JavaScript is disabled in your browser. Here is a step-by-step guide for enabling it on your browser.

Google Chrome

  • Open Chrome
  • In the top right corner,click on and go to Settings.
  • Click on Privacy and security.
  • Go to Site Settings.
  • Click on JavaScript and turn on Allowed(recommended).

Internet Explorer

  • Select Tools Menu.
  • Go to Internet Options.
  • Select the Security tab.
  • Click on the "Custom Level Button..."
  • Scroll down and change the Active Scripting setting by clicking on "Enable".
  • Confirm the change by clicking on "Yes".
  • Press "OK" to close Internet Options.

Mozilla Firefox

  • In the address bar type "about:config" and press Enter.
  • Click on "Accept the Risk and Continue".
  • In the search bar search for "javascript.enabled".
  • Right Click and select "Toggle" or change "false" to "true".
  • Refresh the page.

Microsoft Edge

  • Click on the more button on the top right corner and go to Settings.
  • Search for JavaScript.
  • Click on the search result and turn on Allowed(recommended).
  • JavaScript is enabled.

Opera

  • Open the "Easy Setup" menu.
  • Scroll to the very bottom and click on "Go to Browser Settings".
  • Click on Site Settings or search it in the search bar.
  • Enable JavaScript by clicking on it and turn on Allowed(recommended).

Safari

  • Go to Safari Menu and select "Preferences..."
  • Select the "Security tab".
  • In the "Web Content" section mark the "Enable JavaScript" checkbox .
  • JavaScript is enabled.
